Who is Michael?

Michael Broley is the founder of Caffeine & Anxiety; he helps CEOs, solopreneurs and SMB owners drive revenue by taking control of their personal story. Michael understands that storytelling is integral to developing lasting relationships with clients and building a thriving business!

Before founding Caffeine & Anxiety Michael spent the past 15 years in a variety of sales & marketing roles for companies like Salesforce, Xerox & ballhockey.com. All of these roles challenged him to provide world class customer experiences and to consistently produce and exceed expected results.

An avid photographer, Michael has been freelancing professionally for the past 5 years. This has given him the opportunity to work with brands such as Cineplex Entertainment, LinkedIn, Amber Walker Events, 420 Cannabis Court, and creatives like Simu Liu (Marvel Superhero), Jessica Tyler (Degrassi Alum), Lara Anderson (Tattoo Artist) and Mike Rita (Comedian)!

Michael resides in Mahone Bay, Nova Scotia where he is a proud puppy papa, loves bird watching, and writing terrible poetry.

Michael holds a bachelors degree in Sociology from Western University.
