Who is Michael?

Michael Broley is a freelance creative, full-stack web developer & sales professional. He helps CEOs, solopreneurs and SMB owners drive revenue by taking control of their personal story. Michael understands that storytelling is integral to developing lasting relationships with clients and building a thriving business!

Michael spent the past 20 years in a variety of sales & marketing roles for companies like Salesforce, Xerox & ballhockey.com while also freelancing part-time for a variety of clients. All of these roles challenged him to provide world class customer experiences and to consistently produce & exceed revenue goals! This is where he was able to shape his storytelling skills through a variety of creative initiatves that he spearheaded!

An avid photographer, Michael has been providing services professionally for the past 5 years. This has given him the opportunity to work with brands such as Cineplex Entertainment, LinkedIn, Amber Walker Events, 420 Cannabis Court, and creatives like Simu Liu (Marvel Superhero), Jessica Tyler (Degrassi Alum), Lara Anderson (Tattoo Artist) and Mike Rita (Comedian)!

Michael now resides in Mahone Bay, Nova Scotia where he is a proud puppy papa, loves bird watching, and writing terrible poetry.
